About Us

Welcome to the online counseling platform of RCCG Continent 3 Junior Church, where we are committed to nurturing a generation of children and teenagers in a safe and caring environment.

OUR VISION

To create a safe and nurturing environment where children and teenagers in RCCG Continent 3 Junior Church can find solace, guidance, and support through our counseling service, empowering them to overcome challenges, develop their full potential, and embrace a vibrant relationship with God

Our Mission

OUR MISSION IS TO

SUPPORT

Provide professional, compassionate, and confidential counseling services tailored to the unique needs of Children and Teenagers in RCCG Continent 3 Junior Church, fostering their emotional, spiritual, and psychological well-being.

GUIDE

Offering a safe space for children and teenagers to express themselves, process their experiences, and receive guidance rooted in Christian values.

NAVIGATE

Through our dedicated team of trained counselors, equip young individuals with the tools, skills, and resilience necessary to navigate life's challenges, grow in faith, and develop healthy relationships.

SHAPE

Help everyone thrive as confident and purpose-driven members of their communities, shaping them into positive contributors to society.

Here & Now, Every Day

OUR VALUE ADDED SERVICES

In addition to our counselling services,we offer educational resources,workshops and support groups to help you build resilience,improve communication skills and develop healthy relationships.We also provide referrals to support services if required.
